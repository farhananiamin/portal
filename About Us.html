Our Story

Traveling is more about discovery, less about arrangements. Novastay is your go-to portal when it comes to lodging services across the nation, with our dominant goal of ensuring that your accommodation-booking process is seamless.

Mission

To simplify hotel booking and helping travelers in exploring cities with confidence by providing access to trusted and comfortable accommodations which ensuring a restful and worry-free stay!!

Vision

To become a trusted hotel reservation platform in Malaysia that allows the tourists to travel and rest with comfortable and ease.

Trust & Safety
